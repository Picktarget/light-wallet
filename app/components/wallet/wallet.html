Accounts

    Wallet contracts

    Once you have more than 1 ether you can create more sophisticated contracts. Wallets are one type of basic smart contract, that allows your funds to be controlled by multiple accounts and have an optional daily limit on withdrawals, to increase security. To create your own custom contracts go to the Contracts tab.
